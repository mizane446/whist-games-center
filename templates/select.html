Blackjack

Card game where players aim to get a hand value of 21 or less, competing against the dealer without busting. Face cards count as 10.

Blackjack_eu

European version of the standard Blackjack, where the dealer receives only one card initially and does not check for blackjack until the player has acted.

Whist

Classic four-player trick-taking card game played in fixed partnerships. The team that wins the most tricks scores points, with the first to 5 points victorious.
